4. RETURN & EXCHANGE ELIGIBILITY

Standard Return Policy (30 Days):
Items are eligible for return when:
• Returned within 30 days of delivery date
• Unused, unworn, and in original condition with all tags attached
• Original packaging intact (boxes, wrapping, accessories included)
• Original receipt or order confirmation provided
• Item not damaged or altered by customer

Extended Return Window (60 Days):
• Loyalty program Gold & Platinum members
• Holiday purchases (Nov 1 - Dec 31) extended until January 31
• Defective items - no time limit with proof of purchase

Non-Returnable Items:
• Personal care items (cosmetics, skincare opened/used)
• Intimate apparel and swimwear (hygiene reasons)
• Custom or personalized products
• Digital products and gift cards
• Final sale and clearance items marked "All Sales Final"
• Items without tags or in used condition

Exchange Policy:
• Free exchanges for different size or color within 30 days
• Same-item exchanges processed immediately
• Different-item exchanges treated as return + new purchase
• Exchanges subject to product availability

Defective or Damaged Items:
• Accepted at any time with proof of purchase
• Full refund or replacement guaranteed
• Return shipping covered by us
• Report within 48 hours of delivery for fastest processing

Return Process:
1. Initiate return through your account or contact customer service
2. Print prepaid return label (free for defects/errors, ₹650 for standard returns)
3. Pack item securely in original or similar packaging
4. Drop off at any authorized carrier location
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

Refund Methods:
• Original payment method (standard)
• Store credit with 10% bonus (optional)
• Exchange for different product
• Gift card for same amount

Electronics & Appliances:

Fashion & Apparel:
