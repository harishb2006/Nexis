2. HOW TO RETURN AN ITEM

Easy 5-Step Return Process:

Step 1: Initiate Your Return
• Log into your account at nexisstore.com/returns
• Or email returns@nexisstore.com with order number
• Or call 1-800-NEXIS-HELP
• Have order number and reason for return ready

Step 2: Get Your Return Label
• Receive prepaid return label via email within 1 hour
• Print label at home or show QR code at carrier location
• Standard returns: ₹650 (deducted from refund)
• Defective/wrong items: FREE return label

Step 3: Pack Your Return
• Use original packaging if possible
• Or use any sturdy box that fits the item
• Include all accessories, manuals, and tags
• Do NOT write on original product packaging
• Seal securely with tape

Step 4: Ship Your Return
• Attach return label to outside of package
• Drop off at any authorized carrier location:
  - FedEx Office, Walgreens (for FedEx returns)
  - UPS Store, Staples, CVS (for UPS returns)
  - Any USPS Post Office (for USPS returns)
• Or schedule free home pickup (available for most carriers)
• Save your receipt with tracking number

Step 5: Track & Get Refund
• Track return progress in your account
• We inspect within 2 business days of receipt
• Refund processed within 5-7 business days after inspection
• Receive email confirmation when refund is issued

RETURN FAQS

Q: Do I need the original packaging?
A: Preferred but not required. Any sturdy box works. But original product packaging should be intact (shoe boxes, garment bags, etc.).

Q: Can I return items bought in-store online?
A: Yes! Initiate return online or bring to any store location.

Q: Can I return online purchases in-store?
A: Yes! Bring item with receipt/order confirmation to any store for instant refund or exchange.

Q: What if I lost my receipt?
A: We can look up your order by email, phone, or card used. You'll get store credit for current price.

Q: Can someone else return my order for me?
A: Yes, with order confirmation and ID. Or they can use your return label via mail.

Q: How long until I get my money back?
A: 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your return. Inspection takes 1-2 days.

Q: Can I return sale items?
A: Yes, unless marked "Final Sale." Same 30-day return policy applies.

Q: Do I have to use your return label?
A: No, but recommended. If you use your own shipping, we're not responsible if package is lost.

Q: What if my return is denied?
A: We'll email you why and send item back to you. You can appeal or contact customer service.
