ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

1. MEMBERSHIP ELIGIBILITY
To become a ShopHub member, you must:
- Be at least 18 years of age
- Have a valid email address
- Provide accurate personal information
- Agree to our Terms and Conditions

2. PURCHASE ELIGIBILITY
You are eligible to make purchases if you:
- Have a registered account in good standing
- Provide a valid payment method
- Have a verified shipping address
- Are not currently banned or suspended

3. DISCOUNT ELIGIBILITY
You may be eligible for discounts if you:
- Are a first-time customer (10% off)
- Are a student with valid ID (15% off)
- Are a senior citizen 65+ (10% off)
- Are a member of our loyalty program (5-20% off based on tier)

4. RETURN ELIGIBILITY
Returns are eligible when:
- Items are returned within 30 days of purchase
- Items are unused and in original packaging
- You have the original receipt or order confirmation
- Items are not on the non-returnable list (hygiene products, custom items)

5. PROFESSIONAL DISCOUNT ELIGIBILITY
Professional discounts are available to:
- Healthcare workers (verified through ID.me)
- Teachers and educators (verified with school email)
- Military personnel and veterans (verified through ID.me)
- Non-profit organization employees (verified documentation required)

6. WARRANTY ELIGIBILITY
Products are eligible for warranty if:
- Purchased directly from ShopHub
- Within the warranty period (typically 1-2 years)
- Damage was not caused by misuse or accidents
- You have proof of purchase

For questions about your specific eligibility, please contact our support team.
