2. PAYMENT SECURITY

How We Protect You:

256-Bit SSL Encryption:
• Bank-level encryption on all pages
• Your payment data is encrypted end-to-end
• Look for padlock icon in browser
• All credit card info encrypted in transit

PCI DSS Level 1 Compliance:
• Highest level of payment security certification
• Regular security audits by independent assessors
• Secure card data handling practices
• Industry-standard security protocols

Tokenization:
• We never store your actual card numbers

3D Secure Authentication:

Fraud Detection AI:

• Unusual activity flagged instantly

✗ Store passwords in plain text

Your Responsibilities:

9. PAYMENT ISSUES & TROUBLESHOOTING

Common Payment Failures:

"Payment Declined" - Possible Reasons:
• Insufficient funds
• Incorrect card details (number, CVV, expiration)
• Card expired
• Address mismatch (billing vs. card on file)
• Bank fraud prevention triggered
• Card not activated for online purchases
• Daily spending limit reached

How to Resolve:
1. Verify card details are correct
2. Check with your bank
3. Try different payment method
4. Contact us if problem persists
5. Use PayPal as alternative

"Address Verification Failed":
• Billing address must match card exactly
• Include apartment/unit numbers
• Use address format from card statement
• Try entering address manually vs. autofill
• Contact card issuer to verify address on file

"3D Secure Failed":
• Check phone for verification code
• Ensure pop-ups not blocked
• Try different browser
• Contact bank to enable 3D Secure
• Use alternative payment method

"Transaction Timeout":
• Check if charge went through (don't retry immediately)
• Wait 15 minutes, then try again
• Clear browser cache and cookies
• Try different browser or device
• Contact us to verify no duplicate orders

International Card Issues:
• Enable international transactions with bank
• Check currency conversion settings
• Verify card works for online purchases
• Some banks block foreign merchants initially
• Call bank to whitelist our merchant name
